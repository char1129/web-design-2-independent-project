Hello

Thank you for visiting me! My name is Sarah Charity and I love to create in many ways!
Since I was young, I've always tried to emulate beautiful things by working with my hands. I used to make clothes for my teddy bears, tree forts and ramps to go on make believe adventures, and art. Needless to say, that love to create has followed me into my adult life, each in their own beautiful way. From the arts (music, dance, writing, traditional and digital art, and photography) to working with my hands (auto mechanics and carpentry), and working with my body (working out (running and weight lifting), massage therapy, and yoga).
Creation of self, of the practical and of the arts.
Graphic Design in all its beauty is full of variety. It's many different facids — fields you can branch off to and specialize in is a perfect match for the girl that still loves to create.
This journey first started over a decade ago when I was fresh out of High school. I was not ready. Graphic Design is not for the faint of heart. It takes modivation, dicipline and coffee. After gaining different skills and experience from the workplace, I have returned to finish what I started.

Turns out, I'm good at a few things.

Working in teams.
  Working solo.
   Brainstorming.
    Managing.
     Pitches/Presentations
But, what about software?
